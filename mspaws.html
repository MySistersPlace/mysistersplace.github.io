My Sister's Paws

My Sister's Paws is a program within the women's shelter program of My Sister's Place in Athens, Ohio. The program was founded to serve clients of the women’s shelter to assist them in keeping their pets safe from their abuser so they will leave the abusive environment. During the summer of 2016 an onsite animal shelter will be finished and families will have their pets close by for comfort. If you are leaving an abusive situation, fear for the safety of your pets and do not have relatives or friends to keep them safe, please let the crisis call operator know and discuss your options.

Donate

If you would like to donate to this program there are many ways in which your funds will be used. Food for pets in our care, toys, beds, blankets etc. Most importantly every pet entering our program will be treated for parasites, vaccinated and spayed/neutered. Other medical conditions will also be treated as they arise.

Foster

Sometimes pets need a bit more care than can be provided or we may run out of room. If you would like to serve as a foster for this program please email mysisterspaws@gmail.com We also need volunteers for large animals such as horses.

Sweet Tea Social

Each year since 2012 an event is held in late summer to raise money for this important program. Women gather in their favorite large hats and sun dresses for an afternoon of sweet tea, snacks, libations, raffles, auction items and music. This successful event has made it possible to build an onsite shelter and provide medical care as needed each year. Please join us this year, Sunday, August 28th at 2PM at the Athens Community Center. Tickets are sold in advance and are limited.
